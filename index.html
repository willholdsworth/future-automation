How We Achieve

Our Creative Vision

Our mission is to lead the luxury automation industry, while never compromising on quality or customer service.

State-of-the-art tools, components and knowledge work in cohesion to create an advanced collection of automated and manual mechanisms, designed and manufactured by our teams across the UK and USA.

Every mechanism is individually assembled and tested by hand, and we offer a robust manufacturer warranty on all Future Automation products.

Home

Made-to-order Advantage

All our motorised mechanisms are made to order, you always receive the latest revision, never ageing warehouse stock.

This lean manufacturing ethos frees capacity for more engineers and machinery, enabling us to tackle increasingly complex and elaborate mechanisms.

Every member of our team is trained to an exceptional standard, applied consistently through rigorous quality control at every stage of production.

A History of Innovation

1998
Future Automation founded in Bedford, UK. First product range launches to the residential AV market.
2007
Expansion into commercial and marine sectors. Launch of the first marine-grade motorised TV lift range.
2014
North American office opens in Dover, NH. Product range surpasses 200 individual mechanisms.
